Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026 · Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Belle Trouvé ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application and website at belletrouve.com.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Style preferences — information you provide during onboarding, such as your style quiz responses and fashion interests.
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, including content you view, searches you perform, and items you engage with.
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and app version.
  • Analytics data — aggregated, anonymised usage statistics to help us improve the app.

We do not collect your name, email address, or payment information unless you voluntarily provide it to contact us.

2. Third-Party Services & APIs

Belle Trouvé integrates with the following platforms to deliver content:

  • Pinterest API — used to surface trending fashion accessory pins and boards. Governed by Pinterest's Privacy Policy.
  • Instagram / Meta — used to display trending fashion content. Governed by Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • TikTok — used to surface trending content. Governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Associates — we participate in the Amazon Associates Program. Governed by Amazon's Privacy Notice.
  • Google Firebase & Gemini AI — used for infrastructure and personalised recommendations. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalise your fashion discovery experience based on your style preferences
  • Surface relevant trending content from Pinterest, Instagram, and TikTok
  • Generate personalised style recommendations powered by AI
  • Improve app performance and develop new features
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the app. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us at chporter72@belletrouve.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

5. Children's Privacy

Belle Trouvé is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to opt out of analytics collection. Contact us at chporter72@belletrouve.com to exercise these rights.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
